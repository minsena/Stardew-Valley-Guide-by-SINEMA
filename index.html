🌱 Watch TV Every Morning

Always inspect the television. The fortune teller dictates your daily luck, affecting drop rates in mines, while Cooking shows unlock vital recipes.

🎒 Pierre's Seed Shop

Keep track of Pierre's store schedule. It is closed on Wednesdays initially unless you complete the Community Center restoration.

🍓 Save Gold for Festivals

Save substantial gold during early spring to purchase Strawberry Seeds during the Egg Festival on Spring 13 for massive yields.

🎒 Beginner Farmer Guide

🎒 Expand Inventory Fast

Pierre sells backpack upgrades. Buy the red bag (2,000G / 24 slots) first, followed by the blue bag (10,000G / 36 slots) to avoid discarding valuable ores and items.

❤️ Energy Optimization

Watering crops and tilling earth drains energy. If you reach 0 energy, your farmer gets exhausted, penalty-buffing your starting energy the next morning.

⭐ Level Up Skills

Level up Farming, Mining, Foraging, Fishing, and Combat. Pick professions like Tiller (+10% crop cost) or Forester (+25% wood value) at level 5.

💾 Save Before Sleep

Stardew Valley does not auto-save throughout the day. Your progress is saved only when you sleep in your bed and the summary page finishes.

🌸 Season Calendar

Spring

Cauliflower & Potato

Spring is your starting season. Plant Cauliflowers for giant crops and focus on leveling your agricultural stats early.

Summer

Melons & Starfruit

Summer is hot and profitable. Grow Melons for bundles and buy Starfruit seeds in the Desert for the highest wine profits.

Fall

Pumpkins & Cranberries

Fall features vibrant foliage. Plant Pumpkins for cooking and Cranberries for continuous multiple harvest gains.

Winter

Mining & Tool Upgrades

Standard crops die in winter. Utilize this time to descend the mines, gather wood/coal, and upgrade your watering cans and pickaxes.

💰 Money Making Guide

🌱 Early Game

Pick wild fruits, sell shells collected on the beaches, and fish at the mountain lake for high initial coin rates.

⚙️ Mid Game

Install sprinklers, build preserve jars, and start brewing blueberry and melon wines in wood kegs.

💎 Late Game

Establish a massive Starfruit/Ancient Fruit greenhouse, and utilize basement Casks to age wines to iridium quality.
